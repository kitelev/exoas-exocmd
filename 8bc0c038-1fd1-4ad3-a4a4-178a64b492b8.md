# $nowLocal

Substitution token: current local timestamp in `YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS` format (Asia/Almaty timezone, no offset suffix).

Used for `ems__Effort_endTimestamp`, `ems__Effort_resolutionTimestamp`, `ems__Effort_startTimestamp` writes.
