# Un-archive Ontologically → Asset binding

Binds the "Un-archive Ontologically" command (`14372d1a`) to all `exo__Asset`
instances (inline, `maintenance` group) — the mirror of the forward
[[0fa306a8-44e6-4601-9887-a45c006a7791|Archive Ontologically → Asset binding]].
Universal scope — the inline button appears on every asset whose precondition
[[f1e97b8c-3fe3-4b9a-aa01-16512b7bb8ee|Is ontologically archived]] matches (the
asset's current `isDefinedBy` is the `exo__Ontology_archiveOntology` of some
source ontology, i.e. it was ontologically archived).

`targetClass: exo__Asset` mirrors the forward binding (broadest scope; the
precondition already gates applicability). `order: 911` — placed just after
Archive Ontologically (`910`) within the maintenance group, so the un-archive
button sits next to its forward counterpart.
