# Is terminal status (Done or Trashed)

Reusable **AtomicPrecondition** — returns `true` when the target effort is in a
**terminal** status: `ems__EffortStatusDone` **or** `ems__EffortStatusTrashed`
(req(ems) `127763f8-b97d-472f-872d-7c21ecdaacf6`). Trashed is a second terminal
status equal to Done.

The single source of truth for terminality, used in **both directions**:
- **positively** — one conjunct of `AllPrecondition[Is-terminal, Not-archived]`
  (`7a055a10-e17d-434a-9a2d-8f7c0ba13a4e`), the «Archive Completed» gate (any
  terminal, not-yet-archived effort is archivable);
- **negatively** — wrapped by the «Visible until terminal» NotPrecondition
  (`067e481d-ba2e-4022-a4ec-9e7741a0b7af`), the drop-in replacement for the
  «Visible until Done» atomic (`37517554`) wherever an active-work command must
  hide on a finished effort.

`UNION` (not a `FILTER(?s = A || ?s = B)`) is used for engine-robustness; the
status enum is emitted as its symbolic IRI (`ems#EffortStatusDone` /
`ems#EffortStatusTrashed`), verified against the live vault before ship. Leaf in
≥2 composites → non-vacuous (CQ3).
